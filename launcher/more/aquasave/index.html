About AquaSave

We are dedicated to creating innovative solutions for water conservation and management.

Our Mission

At AquaSave, we believe that every drop counts. Our mission is to develop and implement cutting-edge technologies and strategies that help communities, businesses, and individuals reduce water consumption and preserve this precious resource for future generations.

Founded in 2015, we've helped over 500 organizations reduce their water footprint by an average of 40%, saving billions of gallons of water annually.

Our Services

We offer comprehensive solutions to help you save water and reduce costs.

Residential Solutions

Smart irrigation systems, rainwater harvesting, and water-efficient appliances for your home.

Commercial Solutions

Water auditing, leak detection, and process optimization for businesses and industries.

Agricultural Solutions

Precision irrigation, soil moisture monitoring, and drought-resistant crop consulting.
